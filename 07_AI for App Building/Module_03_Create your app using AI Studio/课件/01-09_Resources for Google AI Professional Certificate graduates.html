01-09 Resources for Google AI Professional Certificate graduates

Congratulations on completing Google AI Professional Certificate! You are eligible to earn a certificate badge from Credly and Google. This badge is a visual representation of a verified credential that you’ve earned. Your dedication has unlocked exclusive career-boosting resources and benefits. 

Claim your resources

To claim these resources at no cost, use the same email address that you used when enrolling in the Google AI Professional Certificate on Coursera. These benefits are only available to graduates who have completed all courses in the Google AI Professional Certificate. Follow the steps below to share your accomplishments and continue expanding your skills:

  • Complete Google AI Professional Certificate (Done!)

  • Claim your Google AI Professional Certificate badge, and share it with your network.

  • You’ll receive an email from Coursera within one week of completing all of the courses in the certificate with instructions on how to claim your certificate badge.

  • Once you’ve opted in, Credly (admin@credly.com) will send your badge.

  • Add the badge to your LinkedIn to highlight your skills! You can follow the steps in this article or video tutorial. 

  • Print and share your Google Career Certificate. After claiming your badge, follow these steps to download, print, and share your Certificate from Google. 

  • Share your accomplishments on social media. Use these sample posts and prompts, and include #GrowWithGoogle to reach a wider audience.

  • Connect with other graduates. Join the AI Professional Community to share how you’re applying your learnings at work, gain career insights, and network with fellow graduates. 

  • Discover new opportunities. Follow Grow with Google on LinkedIn to join a network of people who are interested in gaining AI skills, and explore further learning possibilities.

  • Inspire others by sharing your story. Stories from successful graduates, like you, can inspire the next generation of learners to develop their essential AI skills. In addition, your feedback can help improve the certificate. Fill out this short questionnaire about your journey.

  • Continue to advance your career. Subscribe to email updates from Grow with Google to receive curated career resources, alerts on the latest courses and certificates, and professional tips on utilizing AI. 

  • Explore career possibilities. Career Dreamer is an experimental tool that uses AI to help you uncover your skills and personalized career paths based on your unique experiences and interests. Career Dreamer can help you:

  • Draft a professional story that showcases the value you bring to the workforce.

  • Explore career paths that might align with your skills and interests.

  • Take steps toward your career goals, like creating a cover letter or resume.

  • Navigate your job search efficiently and confidently with AI. The Accelerate Your Job Search with AI course will guide you to use AI to create a job search plan, professional pitch, resume, and more.
