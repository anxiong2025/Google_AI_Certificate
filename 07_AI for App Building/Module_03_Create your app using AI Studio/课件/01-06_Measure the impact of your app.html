01-06 Measure the impact of your app

Congratulations, you successfully completed the capstone project by creating an app! You didn't just map out a workflow. You built a real solution for yourself. You now have the skills and experience to spot opportunities to use AI across all your work. 

What's next? Take your app for a test run and see how it works. 

After you’ve spent time using your new app, ask yourself the following questions:

  • What did I just achieve? How do you feel knowing you just developed a working app without ever writing a line of code? 

  • How much time have I saved? How many hours per week or month did you gain back? 

  • Have I reduced costs? Did this reduce any expenses, like saving money on tools you no longer need or subscription services you replaced? 

  • Has the quality of my work improved?  Are you experiencing fewer errors, better outputs, or higher satisfaction with your work? 

  • Am I more productive? Are you able to complete more tasks or projects? 

  • Did I discover something new? What unexpected benefits did you discover when you used your app?

    • You’ve gained the ability to turn an idea into an app with AI. Take a moment to celebrate and think of ways you can build other solutions.
