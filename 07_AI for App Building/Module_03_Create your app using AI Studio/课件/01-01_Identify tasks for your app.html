01-01 Identify tasks for your app

The first step to building your app is to write down your work goals and all the tasks required to accomplish them. These goals and tasks make up your workflow. Mapping out your workflow will help you identify opportunities to use AI and the type of app that will have the biggest impact on you and your work. 

To begin, you’ll need to fill out the worksheet:

Click here to access the worksheet and create a copy by clicking the “USE TEMPLATE” button. You’ll fill out your copy of the worksheet with your unique goals, tasks, and workflows. Then you’ll rate these tasks to identify where an app can provide the most value.

What you'll gain from completing this worksheet:

  • Clear direction and immediate action: Map your workflow to identify opportunities to use AI, as well as specific tasks where an app can provide the most value.

  • Repeatable problem solving: Evaluate pain points in your workflow and identify opportunities to create an app that can help solve them. 

  • Strategic clarity: Discover where AI can help you do higher quality work faster.

    • As you fill out the worksheet, be specific. The more accurately you map your tasks, the more clarity you’ll have on what app to build.
