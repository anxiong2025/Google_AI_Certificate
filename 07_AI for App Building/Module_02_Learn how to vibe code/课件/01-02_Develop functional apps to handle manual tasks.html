01-02 Develop functional apps to handle manual tasks

In the upcoming labs, Ammaar from Google AI Studio will introduce you to vibe coding. You’ll learn how to use AI Studio to turn ideas into functional apps using natural language. This lets AI handle writing the code so you can focus on creating unique solutions to your workplace challenges.

What you'll gain:

  • Vibe coding experience: Learn how to go from a rough idea to a working app in minutes without writing a line of code.

  • Confidence troubleshooting: Partner with AI to identify and fix bugs quickly.

  • Inspiration to build your own apps: Discover new ways to tackle recurring work challenges and get inspired to make your own apps. 

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the labs, Ammaar will demonstrate how to build three different apps: a tie-breaker app to help you make difficult decisions, a brand builder app to help you prototype marketing assets, and an interactive dashboard to help you better understand, explore, and make informed decisions with your data. As you follow along, think about where these apps could provide value in your day-to-day work. 

    Let’s start building!
