## Q2. Describe the app you created.
> 自由作答（最多 10000 字符）

I built a daily task prioritizer app using Google AI Studio. The app lets users input a list of tasks along with deadlines and estimated effort. It then uses AI to analyze urgency and importance, categorize tasks into a priority matrix, and suggest an optimized daily schedule. It also highlights potential conflicts when too many high-priority tasks overlap on the same day.
