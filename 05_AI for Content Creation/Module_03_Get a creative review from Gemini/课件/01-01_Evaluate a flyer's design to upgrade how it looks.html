01-01 Evaluate a flyer's design to upgrade how it looks

In this lab, Clara from Gemini Marketing will show you how to use Gemini’s multimodal capabilities to review creative assets. Using a mix of text and images, you'll practice multimodal prompting to critique a piece of creative work. And after generating a critique, you’ll draft a note summarizing the feedback. 

What you'll gain: 

  • Specific feedback: Move away from feeling something is off to identifying specific issues

  • Confident decisions: Validate your creative instincts and get concrete suggestions for improvement

  • Better collaboration: Spend less time trying to explain what's wrong and more time moving projects forward with clear communication

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we'll demonstrate how to review a business flyer. However, our technique works for any visual asset where you need to provide feedback. As you follow along, think about the design standards that matter for your place of work, the feedback style your collaborators respond best to, and the improvements your projects need.

    Ready to turn "something feels off" into a clear, actionable note? Let's get started.
