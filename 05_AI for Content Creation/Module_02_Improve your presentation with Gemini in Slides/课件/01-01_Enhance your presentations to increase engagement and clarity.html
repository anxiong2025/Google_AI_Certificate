01-01 Enhance your presentations to increase engagement and clarity

In this lab, Clara from Gemini Marketing will show you how to use Gemini in Google Slides to refine your presentations and generate custom images. You'll learn how to distill key messages, create cohesive visual styles, and build slides that communicate your ideas more effectively.

What you'll gain:

  • Clearer presentations: Transform your slides so your audience can easily scan and understand them

  • Visual consistency: Generate multiple images in a cohesive style without needing design expertise

  • Time savings: Spend less time formatting and designing and more time refining your core message

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we’ll demonstrate how to improve a presentation for a tech services company. However, you’ll get the most out of the lab when you use these techniques to improve your workplace slide decks. Be specific about the style, color, or mission you want to convey in your images.

    Ready to make your presentations more compelling? Let's get started.
