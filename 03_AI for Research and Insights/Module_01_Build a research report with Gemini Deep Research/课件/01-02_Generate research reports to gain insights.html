01-02 Generate research reports to gain insights

In this lab, Mahi from Google DeepMind will show you how Gemini Deep Research can help you gather and synthesize complex information from many sources and turn it into a digestible report. You'll practice strategic research planning, where you’ll collaborate with AI to define the scope, sources, and focus of your research before it begins. You'll start by asking a broad research question. Then, you’ll refine your research plan and generate a report that answers the question.

What you'll gain:

  • Comprehensive answers: Go from a complex question to a detailed report without spending hours browsing

  • Targeted, relevant results: Customize your research plan so it focuses on the exact information, timeframes, and sources you need

  • Shareable insights: Quickly turn dense research into an easy-to-understand infographic that you can share with others

    • Apply what you learn: 

    We’ll demonstrate how to research market trends for a new paint line. However, you can apply this technique to any complex research task. Think about using it to create a competitive analysis, research potential new software to adopt, or learn about the potential impact of new industry regulations. Customize the research plan to fit your specific questions.
