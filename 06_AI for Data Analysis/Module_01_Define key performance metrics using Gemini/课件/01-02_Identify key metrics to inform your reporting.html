01-02 Identify key metrics to inform your reporting

In this lab, Owen from Google Marketing will show you how to use Gemini to elevate your data reporting and preparation. You'll collaborate with Gemini to decide which metrics to focus on, to anticipate stakeholder questions, and to get step-by-step help when you’re pulling your data.

What you'll gain:

  • Strategic focus: Quickly zero in on the data that matters most 

  • Greater confidence: Lead the data conversation by preparing for stakeholder questions

  • Tool guidance: Get clear, step-by-step help with any data tool, eliminating technical roadblocks

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we’ll demonstrate how to prepare for an executive review at an e-commerce company. However, to get the most out of this lab, consider your work and the most important things you need to track. As you follow along, try adapting the prompts to your own industry, your specific goals, and the people you report to.

    Let's get you ready for your next data presentation!
