01-01 Analyze spreadsheet data using everyday language

In this lab, Owen from Google Marketing will show you how Gemini in Google Sheets can help you turn raw data into actionable insights. You'll analyze data in a spreadsheet, generate narratives to explain trends, and build a chart, all without writing a single complex formula. 

What you'll gain:

  • Insights without formulas: Get powerful data analysis even if you’re not a spreadsheet expert

  • Clear communication: Instantly generate charts and explanations to help others understand the data

  • Faster decision making: Move from raw data to strategic insight in minutes

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we’ll demonstrate how to analyze e-commerce marketing performance. However, to get the most out of the lab, try adapting the prompts to analyze and visualize your sales figures, project data, or customer feedback. 

    Important: Always make sure that you’re using AI responsibly. Avoid including private, sensitive, or confidential data in your prompts.
