# AI for Brainstorming and Planning — Course Challenge

## Q1. The owner of a fitness center wants to introduce new classes to attract new members. From the options below, what is the most effective way to use Gemini to brainstorm a wide variety of class ideas?
- ○ Generate a cost-benefit analysis for the gym's existing class schedule and include the range of potential revenue and operational costs.
- ○ Create a customer survey to find out what fitness activities the existing members currently enjoy doing.
- ● **Use a fitness expert persona to generate 10 innovative class concepts for different fitness levels.** ✅
- ○ Schedule a recurring weekly report on new fitness equipment trends.

## Q2. A business owner has a list of 10 potential software vendors. They need a structured way to compare them. From the options below, what is the most effective way to use Gemini to evaluate these options against a consistent framework?
- ○ Produce a spreadsheet from the vendor list and create a chart to share with your team.
- ○ Research and summarize the latest trends in the enterprise software market.
- ● **Analyze all 10 vendors in a table with columns for costs and key benefits.** ✅
- ○ Use an IT expert persona to brainstorm 5 more potential vendors.

## Q3. A boutique owner used Gemini to review the plan for a holiday pop-up shop and identified a missing dependency: Staff training on the new cash register and credit card system. They now need to understand the impact of this missing step. From the options below, what is the most effective next step to use Gemini as a strategic collaborator?
- ○ Create a list of interview questions for hiring temporary holiday staff.
- ● **Determine if the November 1st opening is at risk because of training time.** ✅
- ○ Draft an email to the staff explaining how the new cash register and credit card system works.
- ○ Summarize the credit card system's technical manual into a one-page reference sheet.

## Q4. A business owner is onboarding a new employee and has several key documents (e.g., HR guides, project plans). They want the new hire to find information independently. From the options below, what is the most effective way to set up a reliable, centralized information source?
- ○ Email all the documents as attachments to the new hire's inbox.
- ● **Upload all onboarding documents to a new NotebookLM hub and share it with the new hire.** ✅
- ○ Ask Gemini to summarize all documents into a single new memo.
- ○ Upload the documents to NotebookLM and ask it to identify risks in the HR guides.

## Q5. After a product manager uploaded a plan for launching a new app, Gemini identified a constraint: there is no time allocated for user testing. From the options below, what is the most effective next step to use Gemini as a strategic collaborator to manage this new constraint?
- ● **Analyze the impact of adding a two-week testing phase on the final launch date.** ✅
- ○ Schedule a recurring reminder for the manager to create a test plan.
- ○ Upload the project plan to a hub for the testing team to manage the constraint.
- ○ Brainstorm a list of potential bugs the testing phase might find.
