01-02 Build a knowledge hub to answer questions instantly

In this lab, Owen from Google Marketing will show you how to use NotebookLM to create a centralized knowledge hub that’s grounded in your specific sources. Your team will be able to query it using natural language. NotebookLM is useful for when you need to create a shared knowledge hub where your team can instantly find accurate answers directly from your project documents.

What you'll gain:

  • Instant, accurate answers: Give your team a way to find information without waiting for a reply

  • Reduced interruptions: Spend less time answering routine questions and more time on focused work

  • A source of truth: Ensure everyone on the team is working from the same information, based only on the documents you provided

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we’ll demonstrate how to build a hub for a specific project launch. However, you’ll get the most impact when you apply these techniques to your own work. For example, you could use this to onboard a new team member with HR documents and team guides, or to give your sales team a virtual expert who will help them get up to speed quickly. As you follow along, think about what documents you’d upload to unblock or support your team.
