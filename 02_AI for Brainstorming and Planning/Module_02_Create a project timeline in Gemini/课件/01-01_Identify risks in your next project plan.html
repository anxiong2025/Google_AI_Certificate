01-01 Identify risks in your next project plan

In this lab, Owen from Google Marketing will show you how to use Gemini to analyze project plans and identify hidden risks. You'll practice giving Gemini the context, background information, and clear instructions it needs to act as a strategic thought partner. You’ll start with a project plan, prompt Gemini to poke holes in it, and generate an updated timeline that sets you up for success. 

What you'll gain: 

  • Improved risk management: Catch potential problems before they derail your project 

  • Deeper analysis: Get a comprehensive review of your plan in minutes instead of hours 

  • Clearer communication: Create timelines that account for all the moving parts and show how they connect so you can confidently share them with your team

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we'll demonstrate how to analyze a product launch plan for headphones. However, you’ll get the most out of the lab when you apply these techniques to your own work. As you follow along, think about adapting the prompts to your specific projects, deadlines, and planning needs.

    Let's turn your plans into action with fewer surprises along the way!
