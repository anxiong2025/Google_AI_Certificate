01-02 Get feedback to strengthen your work

In this lab, Clara from Gemini Marketing will show you how to use Gemini to simulate stakeholder perspectives and identify gaps in your thinking. You'll collaborate with AI and give it specific personas and contexts so it can challenge your proposals the same way real stakeholders would. You'll upload a draft proposal, receive targeted feedback, and refine your work based on multiple viewpoints.

What you'll gain:

  • Increased confidence: Walk into important meetings feeling prepared for tough questions

  • Stronger proposals: Identify blind spots and strengthen your ideas before you present them

  • Diverse viewpoints: Understand different perspectives by testing your idea against multiple personas

    • Apply what you learn: 

    In the lab, we’ll demonstrate how to review a proposal from a logistics company. However, you’ll get the most out of the lab by applying our techniques to your own circumstances. As you follow along, try swapping in your own documents and experimenting with different stakeholder personas relevant to your job.

    Let's get ready to turn tough questions into confident answers!
